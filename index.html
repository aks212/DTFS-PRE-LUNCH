Frequently Asked Questions

DTFS is a platform offering secure, non-interest trade financing solutions that connect businesses with easy, transparent, and flexible financial tools.

Registering early gives you exclusive access to the platform, priority onboarding, and special offers, including discounted fees and APY earnings on transactions.

We use blockchain technology and smart contracts to ensure that all transactions are transparent, secure, and automated, eliminating manual errors and fraud.

Our platform's minimum requirements include: a registered business entity, 6+ months of operational history, and basic KYC documentation. We assess each application based on transaction history and business metrics rather than traditional collateral.

DTFS token holders receive multiple benefits including reduced platform fees, priority access to new features, governance voting rights, and a share in platform revenue through our unique profit-sharing model. Early adopters receive additional token allocations and exclusive benefits.

DTFS implements multiple layers of security including smart contract audits by leading firms, multi-signature wallets for fund management, automated escrow systems, and regular security updates. All transactions are recorded on the blockchain for maximum transparency and security.

For KYC verification, businesses need to provide:

  • Business registration/incorporation documents
  • Tax identification numbers
  • Proof of business address (utility bill, lease agreement)
  • Director and beneficial owner identification (passport/ID)
  • Recent bank statements (last 3 months)
  • Trading licenses and permits (if applicable)

The verification process typically takes 24-48 hours once all documents are submitted.
